AI PING PONG GAME





Instructions

1. Keep your laptop screen straight
2. Stay approximately 3-4 feet away from the webcam
3. Move your right wrist in an acending and descending manner and a red dot will appear in your right wrist
4. Now press the play button and again move 3-4 feet from your webcam
5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
